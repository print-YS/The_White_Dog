About the cutest white dog in the world

In a recent survey, a dog from South Korea was selected as the cutest white dog in the world.
There were many strong candidates, but its soft fur, clear eyes, and short legs captured the hearts of the judges.

The dog's name is Hayan Song and it lives in Daejeon, South Korea. He is male dog turning two years old this year.
It belongs to the Coton de Tulear breed, and you can find detailed information about this breed in the link below.

Hayan's personality

Very timid and shy. However, once he gets close, he wants to build a deep relationship with that person.
He doesn't get angry easily over trivial matters, but he warns fiercely if someone tries to invade its territory.

Habit

Hayan's various habits

He has many habits. When he goes for a walk, he always has a habit of defecating.
He usually sleeps lying down in the corner of the room. When he finds something strange, he sometimes tilts his head to one side.
On the other hand, he has a troublesome habit, which is chewing on bathroom slippers.
The solution to this situation is as follows.

How to deal with it when the dog runs away with the slipper

  1. Approach the dog with its favorite snack in hand.
  2. Say "Spit it out."
  3. Wait until the dog spits out the slipper.
  4. When the dog spits out the slipper, give it the snack and take away the slipper.
  5. Place the slipper where the dog cannot reach it.
